Questions from Godfrey

Do you offer emergency furnace repair in Godfrey?

Yes, a dead furnace in winter is an emergency and we treat it that way. Our hours are Monday through Friday 7am to 6pm plus Saturday 8am to 2pm. Godfrey is well inside our normal service area, and most repairs get handled the same day because we stock common parts on the truck.

How do I decide between fixing my furnace and buying a new one?

Depends on its age, its condition, and what the repair costs against what the furnace has left in it. We'll tell you straight when a repair still makes sense and when you'd be putting money into a unit that's done. Quotes are free, so it costs nothing to find out.
