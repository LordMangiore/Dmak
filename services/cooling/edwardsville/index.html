Questions from Edwardsville

How fast can you fix an AC in Edwardsville?

Most calls get same-day service, we're based right in town and keep common parts stocked on the truck. During heat waves we prioritize the homes with no cooling at all and get to them as fast as we can. Call (314) 420-9851 and you get Dan, not a call center.

My AC runs but the house never cools down. What's wrong?

Could be low refrigerant, a weak capacitor, dirty coils, or a system that was undersized to begin with. We'll diagnose it and quote the fix for free, and if the real problem is the unit itself, we'll tell you that straight. No pressure either way.
