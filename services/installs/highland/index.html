Questions from Highland

Do you install new HVAC systems in Highland, or just handle repairs?

Both. We install furnaces, heat pumps, and complete AC systems in Highland and across the Metro East. In-home estimates are free, and financing options are available on installs.

How do I know what size furnace or AC my house needs?

We run the numbers on your actual house, meaning square footage, insulation, windows, and ductwork, instead of matching whatever was in there before. An oversized system short-cycles and an undersized one never catches up. Getting the size right is most of the job.
