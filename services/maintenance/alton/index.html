Questions from Alton

When should I schedule a furnace or AC tune-up in Alton?

Cooling in spring, heating in fall, before the season leans on the system. Fall calendars fill up fast once the first cold night hits, so booking early is easier on everyone. A tune-up costs a lot less than the breakdown it heads off.

What happens if my system breaks down between tune-ups?

Call us. Most repair calls get fixed the same day since we stock common parts on the truck, and tune-up customers get priority on the schedule. Tune-ups make breakdowns rarer, but nothing makes them impossible.
