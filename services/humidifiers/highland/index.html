Questions from Highland

Do you install whole-home humidifiers in Highland?

Yes, we install and service whole-home humidifiers throughout Highland and the surrounding Metro East. Most units tie into your existing furnace, and it's often easy to pair the install with a seasonal tune-up. Quotes are free, so it costs nothing to find out what your house needs.

Will a whole-home humidifier cause mold?

Not if it's sized and set correctly. The horror stories come from oversized units and set-points left too high during a cold snap. We match the unit to your house, dial in the settings, and show you how to adjust it as the weather changes.
