Questions from Alton

Are whole-home humidifiers worth it in an older house?

Usually, yes, older homes leak air, so in winter the dry outside air keeps pulling your humidity down. Bringing it back up protects wood floors and trim, cuts down on static and dry sinuses, and makes the same thermostat setting feel warmer.

Do you install whole-home humidifiers in Alton?

We do. Alton is part of our everyday service area, and most humidifier installs tie into your existing furnace in a single visit. Quotes are free, so it costs nothing to find out whether your system is a good fit.
