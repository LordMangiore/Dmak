Questions from Troy

Do you install whole-home humidifiers in Troy, IL?

We do. We install and service whole-home humidifiers and air quality equipment throughout the Metro East, and Troy is a short drive from our Edwardsville shop. Quotes are free.

Is a whole-home humidifier better than portable units?

For a whole house, yes. It mounts on your ductwork, uses the furnace fan you already run, and keeps the entire home at a set humidity without daily refills. Portables are fine for one room, but they can't keep up with a full house in winter.
